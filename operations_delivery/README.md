# Operations and Delivery Dashboard

## Overview

This report provides a comprehensive view of operational performance and delivery activities. It is designed to help operations managers and logistics teams monitor day to day activities, track delivery outcomes, and identify areas that require attention or improvement.

## Purpose

The dashboard serves as a central monitoring tool for operations and delivery teams. It enables stakeholders to track order fulfilment, delivery timelines, and operational efficiency in order to maintain high service standards and meet business targets.

## Contents

This Power BI report covers the following areas:

**Operations Overview**
A high-level summary of operational activity, including total orders processed, orders in progress, and completed operations within a selected time period.

**Delivery Performance**
A breakdown of delivery outcomes, including on-time deliveries, delayed deliveries, and failed delivery attempts across different regions or routes.

**Order Fulfilment**
An analysis of the order fulfilment process from order placement to final delivery, highlighting bottlenecks and processing times.

**Regional Delivery Breakdown**
A geographic view of delivery activity showing performance across different zones, cities, or distribution areas.

**Time-Based Trends**
Visual representations of operational and delivery performance over time, allowing teams to spot patterns, peak periods, and recurring issues.

**Key Performance Indicators**
Core operational metrics, including delivery success rate, average delivery time, order processing time, and fulfilment rate versus targets.

## Data Source

The data used in this report is sourced from internal operations management systems and delivery tracking platforms covering all active delivery routes and fulfilment centres.

## How to Use

Open the file in Microsoft Power BI Desktop to interact with the dashboard. Use the filters and slicers provided on each page to explore data by region, time period, delivery status, or operational unit.

## Requirements

Microsoft Power BI Desktop is required to open and interact with this file. It can be downloaded for free from the official Microsoft website.
