# Retail Sales Report

## Overview

This report provides a comprehensive analysis of retail sales performance. It is designed to give users a clear picture of sales trends, product performance, and revenue distribution across different dimensions of the business.

## Purpose

The report serves as a decision support tool for retail managers, sales teams, and business analysts. It enables stakeholders to monitor sales performance, identify top performing products, and make informed business decisions based on data.

## Contents

This Power BI report covers the following areas:

**Sales Performance**
A summary of overall sales figures including total revenue, units sold, and average transaction value over a selected period.

**Product Analysis**
A breakdown of sales by product category and individual product, highlighting best selling and underperforming items.

**Regional Sales**
A view of sales distribution across different store locations or regions, identifying areas of strong and weak performance.

**Time Based Trends**
Visual representations of sales over time including daily, monthly, and yearly comparisons to track growth and seasonal patterns.

**Customer Insights**
An overview of purchasing behavior including frequency of purchases and revenue contribution by customer segment.

**Key Performance Indicators**
Core retail metrics including gross revenue, net sales, profit margins, and sales targets versus actuals.

## Data Source

The data used in this report is sourced from internal retail sales records and transaction databases covering store and online sales channels.

## How to Use

Open the file in Microsoft Power BI Desktop to interact with the report. Use the filters and slicers provided on each page to explore data by product, region, time period, or sales channel.

## Requirements

Microsoft Power BI Desktop is required to open and interact with this file. It can be downloaded for free from the official Microsoft website.
